Overview

Purpose

We want to let people know that white water rafting is for all ages and all skill levels. If you go out with the correct equipment and follow the guidelines of your guide, then river rafting is a safe and awesome sport!

Audience

If you are wanting to try this awesome outdoor sport for the first time or you know the river like the back of your hand, we have all of the equipment you need to make your experience thrilling! We have something for all ages and all skill levels.
