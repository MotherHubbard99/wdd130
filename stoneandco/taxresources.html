Internal Revenus Services (IRS)

IRS.gov provides information about federal income and business taxes. It is a good place to learn more about various forms, deductions, tax rates, and to find out when you should receive your refund.

Utah State Tax Commision

The Utah State Tax Commission administers state tax laws and collects tax revenue. It is also the agency responsible for motor vehicle registration and titles.

Where's My Refund?

Good question!! You can use the "Where's My Refund" tool to find out when you will receive your refund. To use this tool, you will need to know your filing status, Social Security Number, and the amount of your federal tax refund.

You can use the Utah online "Where's My Refund" tool to find out when you will receive your refund from the State of Utah. To use this tool, you will need to know your Social Security Number, expected Utah refund amount or federal adjusted gross income (FAGI) listed on your Utah return.

Tax Return Due Dates

Remember, your 2023 Individual Income tax returns and payments are due on April 18, 2023. If you file after this date, you may have to pay interest and penalties. Please visit the IRS.gov website for more information about interest and penalties on your federal tax return. For information on Utah interest and penalties, please visit the Utah State Tax Commission website.

Other Filing Deadlines

Partnership tax returns - Wednesday, March 15, 2023
S Corporation tax returns - Tuesday, March 15, 2023.
