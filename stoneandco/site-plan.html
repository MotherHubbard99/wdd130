Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to get the tax office, of Stone and Company, a presence on the internet. Firstly, it will show up in the search for a tax office near me and hopefully gain more clients every year. Secondly, the site will have a tax organizer on it that is readily available to download for individual, rental and business returns; so the client knows which documents to bring in for their appointment.

Audience

Any person who has to file a tax return.

    Tax Resources

    Internal Revenue Services (IRS)

    IRS.gov provides information about federal income and business taxes. It is a good place to learn more about various forms, deductions, tax rates, and to find out when you should receive your refund.

    Utah State Tax Commission

    The Utah State Tax Commission administers state tax laws and collects tax revenue. It is also the agency responsible for motor vehicle registration and titles.

    Where's My Refund?

    Good question!! You can use the "Where's My Refund" tool to find out when you will receive your refund. To use this tool, you will need to know your filing status, Social Security Number, and the amount of your federal tax refund.
    You can use the Utah online "Where's My Refund" tool to find out when you will receive your refund from the State of Utah. To use this tool, you will need to know your Social Security Number, expected Utah refund amount or federal adjusted gross income (FAGI) listed on line 4 of your Utah return.

    Tax Return Due Dates

    Remember, your 2022 Individual Income tax returns and payments are due on April 15, 2023. If you file after this date, you may have to pay interest and penalties. Please visit the IRS.gov website for more information about interest and penalties on your federal tax return. For information on Utah interest and penalties, please visit the Utah State Tax Commission website.
